About Me

My name is Nick Ward and I'm learning to become a computer programmer. Don't let this profile picture fool you into thinking I have elite skills. I am but a simple sales rep at my current job with little to no experience programming.

I was born in a paper sack, in the bottom of a sewer. I had to eat dirt clumps for breakfast because my family was so poor. My dad was a waitress and my mom sold bathroom tiles. My brothers and sisters all hated me because I was an only child. My parents sold me for a nickel when I'm clearly worth a dime. Clocks cost more then a nickel so I wasn't worth their time.

Now that I've become old, I enjoy long YouTube videos of people walking on the beach and the fresh air when I let my pug go out for a pee.
